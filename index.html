David Holly

David Holly (born 3 October 1992) is a graphic designer and illustrator working with English football clubs at Regen Graphics FC. David loves football and is passionate about local grassroots sport, having made a handful of appearences in the Northern League.

David is working to climb the football pyramid by working with clubs in a variety of divisions and ultimately, represent his country England.
